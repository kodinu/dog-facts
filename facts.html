Facts about dogs:

Dogs can smell a million times better than humans.
Dogs can smell feelings.
Dogs can hear four times as far as humans.
Dogs are emotional beings just like humans.
Dogs dream like humans and about similar things.
Dogs can make about 10 different vocal sounds.
Dogs sweat through their paws.
Three dogs survived the Titanic.
A dog‘s bad breath might be sign of poor health.
Dogs can sense storms coming before they happen.
Dogs don’t like rain because the amplified sound hurts their sensitive ears.
Dogs are as smart as a two-year-old baby.
Puppies are born blind and deaf.
Dogs only mate twice a year.
Dogs have a "sixth sense".
45% of dogs sleep in their owner’s bed.
There are hundreds of different breeds of dogs.
When dogs kick after going to the bathroom, they are using the scent glands on their paws to further mark their territory.
Labradors have been the most popular breed in the United States for the last 26 years.
